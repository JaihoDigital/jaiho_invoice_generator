Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our invoice generator and services.

Jaiho InvoiceGen is a free, web-based invoice generator that helps businesses create professional invoices quickly and easily. It supports multiple currencies, tax calculations, and allows you to download invoices as PDF files.

Yes, our basic invoice generator is completely free to use. You can create unlimited invoices, download them as PDF, and print them without any cost. We also offer premium features for advanced users.

Our invoice generator includes: multiple tax calculation methods (global, per-item, common), support for multiple currencies, logo upload, digital signature options, customizable company and client details, automatic calculations, PDF export, and print functionality.

Yes, you can easily upload and add your company logo to invoices. Simply click on the logo placeholder in the invoice preview and select your logo file. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and other common image formats.

No, we do not store your invoice data on our servers. All invoice creation happens locally in your browser, ensuring your business information remains private and secure. Your data is only saved when you explicitly download the invoice.

Our invoice generator works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience and performance.

We support three tax calculation methods: Global (applies one tax rate to the total), Per-Item (different tax rates for each item), and Common (multiple tax components like CGST/SGST). The common tax method calculates taxes on the final taxable amount (subtotal minus discount).

Yes, you can apply discounts to your invoices. Simply enter the discount amount in the discount field, and it will be automatically deducted from the subtotal before tax calculations. The discount will be clearly shown on the invoice.
